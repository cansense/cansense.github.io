BIOTELEMETRY

Cansense Animal Biotelemetry Solutions are a comprehensive set of latest technological adaptations and tools for animal welfare, species conservation and behaviour study. These solutions are easily adaptable to both confined as well as wild environments. Cansense Animal Biotelemetry solutions are equally adaptable for the management of Wildlife, Marinelife as well as livestock. A new set of measurements that Cansense offers is Health Monitoring and Health Management. These are Realtime and precise measurement of health vitals. A combination of Wildlife sciences, sensing technologies and communicationtechnologies make Cansense a scientifically preferred option all over the World. In direct contrast to the general perception that prevails, Cansense solutions are guaranteed to be cost effect and generate more scientific data thereby eliminating and or reducing the degree of error in data acquisition.
